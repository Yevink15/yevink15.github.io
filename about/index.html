About the Photographer

I'm Yevin Kariyawasam, the photographer behind YKCaptures. I cover collegiate esports, sports, campus events, festivals, and live moments where timing matters as much as composition.

I'm actively involved in collegiate esports and serve as the Valorant Manager at GMU Esports, which gives me a close read on tournament flow, player reactions, and the moments teams actually care about having documented.

Professionally, I work as a Systems Engineer at Northrop Grumman. Outside of engineering, I bring that same systems mindset to photo delivery: organized galleries, consistent edits, and quick turnaround.
